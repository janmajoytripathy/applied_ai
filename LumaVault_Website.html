About LumaVault

LumaVault is an SaaS company developing the next generation of cloud-based data backup, storage, and collaboration solutions. Our product is built on three core principles:

  • Governance, Security, and Privacy: Secure encrypted cloud storage.
  • Collaboration: Seamless file synchronization across devices.
  • Availability: Multi-platform support (MacOS, Windows, Linux, Android, iOS).

Key Features

  • Secure, encrypted storage with seamless synchronization.
  • Integration with all popular cloud solutions, SaaS, and IaaS providers.
  • Custom synchronization pipelines through an easy-to-use API.
  • AI-driven document scanning and indexing with iLLUMAnate™.
  • SynergyIndex™ to detect collaboration opportunities within your organization.

Innovative AI Technology

Our proprietary iLLUMAnate™ functionality uses AI to scan, index, and match documents with enterprise policies, identifying areas of non-compliance before they become breaches.

SynergyIndex™ helps managers detect duplication of efforts and collaboration opportunities among employees.

By leveraging the power of AI, LumaVault ensures secure, compliant, and smart data management.
