About LumaVault

LumaVault is an SaaS company developing the futuristic cloud-based data and backup solution, storage, and collaborative based solutions. Our products are based and build on three core principles:

  • Governance, Security, and Privacy: Secure encryption of storage.
  • Collaboration: Seamless file sync and indexing across devices.
  • Availability: Multi-platform support (MacOS, Windows, Linux, Android, iOS).

Key Features

  • Cloud-based solution that is secure, encrypted storage for organizations to backup and synchronize their data with clients across a variety of platforms.
  • LumaVault also seamlessly integrates with all popular cloud solutions, including SaaS and IaaS providers
  • Simple-to-use API ecosystem to support development of custom synchronization pipelines.
  • iLLUMAnate™ : An AI-led document scanning and indexing software
  • SynergyIndex™: A collaboration tool to reduce redundancies acoss your organization.

Innovative AI Technology

Our proprietary iLLUMAnate™ functionality uses advanced AI systems to scan documents and index them, unlocks new possibilities for collaboration and data governance. With this technology, LumaVault can automatically scan documents and cross-match them with enterprise privacy and security policies to identify potential areas of non-compliance before they become breaches.
